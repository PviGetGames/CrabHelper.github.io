Welcome to the 90's, a simpler time.

Talk with your friends in this safe retro chat platform.

No messages are logged and freedom of speech is guaranteed!
Using the Internet Relay Chat protocol, with no third party services, extreme privacy is ensured.

Click connect to begin chatting now!

For guidence on how to use this platform, click the Commands tab for a list of things you can do.

Disclaimer: Once everyone leaves a channel, it automatically gets deleted.
This happens because no third party service was added to ensure channels staying up forever.
If you want to reserve a channel, please contact LexIRC Helpdesk and I will set up a user account, that
cannot read messages, to stay online and keep your channel open.
Uptime highly depends on the amount of users present on the IRC Network.

Kiwi IRC is the current service being used for the webclient.
Kiwi IRC does not store/log messages and does not display your IP address.
Kiwi IRC masks your IP address with theirs (your location will appear in France).

However, a personal client does not do that.
If you do not wish to have your IP address seen by other users while using a personal client,
I would advise using a trustworthy VPN service, such as NordVPN and ExpressVPN.
